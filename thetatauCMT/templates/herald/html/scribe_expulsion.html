Roll Book Update


Dear Chapter Scribe,

The Executive Council voted on {{ date }} to expel {{ user }}, badge number: {{ badge_number }} from the Fraternity. The National Fraternity’s records have been updated, but as Scribe, it is your responsibility to update your chapter’s roll book to indicate this former member’s expulsion.

To update your roll book, please follow guidance from the Policies & Procedures Manual (PPM) Section 2.6.1.3 and 7.2.5. Specifically, use a permanent pen (that does not bleed through to the record on the back of the page) to place a single strikethrough on the expelled member’s roll book page and annotate the expulsion date from above. In the page margin, record the reason(s) for the expulsion.

Once completed, initial and date the annotated remarks and include your chapter name and your roll number so the future members of the chapter can identify who authorized and made the comments. Roll book pages shall not be destroyed nor damaged such that any information is illegible.

Please respond to central.office@thetatau.org when this action has been completed.

If you have questions, please reach out to the Central Office.
