Incomplete Tasks:

Tasks are to help the officers of the chapter to remember to complete tasks in a timely manner. Some tasks have forms/submissions associated with them. You should try to complete the form or submit the appropriate document before marking the task complete.

Sometimes it does not make sense to submit a form (eg. no prealumn members this semester, so no need to submit a pre-alumn form or "Our chapter does not do lock-ins"). In those cases, simply click the "Mark Complete" button in the task page, as you acknowledge the task no longer needs to be completed.
